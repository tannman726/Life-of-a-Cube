About


Life of a cube was made in 2019 by 16 year old Tanner Nuss. With story and artistic help by Alex Contreras.

Real data about death chances of an average 'Earthican' excluding unnatural causes (third party injury, disasters etc). This simulation shows that if you had a cube kid in a isolated world, how likely he/she would be to surive to 122 years old.

Back Story:

“You ever hear the story? The story of 4 sides? Welp if you haven’t.. gamers rise up! This is the story of “Cuboid the Cube” All he wanted in life was to be normal, he wanted to eat, sleep, drink, and do all the things regular humans could do. That was his lifelong dream.. One day Cuboid came across a gang known as the “Quadrangles” a well known street gang of Quadrilaterals known for their mischiefs and scammers. Cuboid went up to the leader of the Quadrangles and wanted to become a normal being. The Quadrangles agreed, but being the scam artist they were. They changed Cuboids life forever They made him normal BUT! He could not -eat -sleep -drink -and he was still a Cube. Cuboid now faces many dangers, including death. Will you help our friend Cuboid resecure his life, or will he crumble under the control of the Quadrangles? That’s for you to decide! Now..help Cuboid and find his destiny!
